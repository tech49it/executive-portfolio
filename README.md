## Current Engagement — Atrium Platform

**Role: Principal Architect · BI Lead · AI Product Strategy**

Atrium is a production multi-tenant enterprise SaaS platform for luxury residential property operations. It replaces fragmented, paper-and-email workflows with an integrated operations and intelligence layer spanning valet, EV charging, car wash, front desk, service recovery, workforce management, parking, and damage claims.

**What I own:**
- System architecture — multi-tenant data model, RBAC, Cloud Functions backend, Firestore operational store, BigQuery analytics pipeline
- Product roadmap — across 8 operational modules, dual-interface application (Command Center + Resident Portal)
- AI implementation — service triage engine, intelligent routing, automated SLA escalation, AI-generated executive summaries
- BI architecture — KPI framework, executive dashboard design, department and vendor scorecards
- Integration design — notification dispatch layer (Email, SMS, Push), EV hardware API, vendor workflow integrations

## Philosophy

The purpose of enterprise technology is to improve the quality of decisions — not to demonstrate technical sophistication. I design every system with a single question in front of me: what decision does this enable, and for whom?

Architecture follows from that answer. Never the other way around.
